I do not think that page rank of B will change for a different choice of p
in the range of 0 <= p <= 1 because I have already tested the data for
values of p = 0.2, p = 0.5, p = 0.8. The data has not changed in those 
three times, therefore it does not seem likely that the data will change
for any other choice of p.

Another reason for the pagerank probably not changing is that it appears
that all of the nodes are equally pointing in & out of each other, so they
will all rank the same regardless of the p damping factor.
